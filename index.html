Biography

I have received my Ph.D. degree in Computational Mathematics from University of Science and Technology of China in 2022, supervised by Prof. Ligang Liu and Prof. Xiao-Ming Fu. Before that, I also received my Bachelor of Science from Northeastern University in 2017.

Research interests

  • Geometric Processing and Optimization.
  • Computer-aided Geometric Design.
  • Digital Content Creation.
